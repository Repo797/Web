Introducing Scoop 2.0!

We are more than a question paper app now!

Scoop Docs is a document submission and sharing feature that enables students to upload and share academic materials like notes, assignments, and exam papers. Users can organize content by department, course, semester, and document type, making it easy to find relevant resources. Uploaded documents are subject to an approval process to ensure quality before becoming available to others. Once approved, these documents can be viewed by users within the app.

Scoop Buzz is a platform within the Scoop app that keeps users informed with the latest updates, news, and announcements. Users can also submit their own "Buzz" to share important news or updates with the community, encouraging active participation and making it a dynamic hub for relevant information.
