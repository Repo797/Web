Introducing Scoop 2.0!

We are more than a question paper app now!

Scoop Docs lets students submit and share academic materials like notes, assignments, and exam papers. Users can organize content by department, course, and semester for easy access. Documents go through an approval process to ensure quality before becoming available to others, promoting peer learning and efficient resource sharing.

Scoop Buzz is a feature within the Scoop app that keeps users updated with the latest news and announcements. It presents important information like academic updates and events in a user-friendly format. Users can also contribute by submitting their own "Buzz," fostering active participation and creating a dynamic hub for community-driven information.
