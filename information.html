We are more than a question paper app now!

Scoop Docs is a document submission and sharing feature that enables students to upload and share academic materials like notes, assignments, and exam papers. Users can organize content by department, course, semester, and document type, making it easy to find relevant resources. Uploaded documents are subject to an approval process to ensure quality before becoming available to others. Once approved, these documents can be viewed by users within the app. The feature promotes peer learning and content sharing, helping students access useful study materials in a structured and efficient manner.

Scoop Buzz is a platform within the Scoop app that keeps users informed with the latest updates, news, and announcements. It offers a user-friendly experience, presenting important information such as academic updates, events, and other relevant content. Along with staying updated, users also have the ability to contribute by submitting their own "Buzz" to share important news or updates with the community. This feature encourages active participation, making Scoop Buzz a dynamic hub for relevant information and peer-driven contributions.
